About Pecado da Gula

Pecado Da Gula is both a well known destination eatery and well kept secret amongst lovers of fine food and lovely venues. Set in rolling gardens under shady trees, the restaurant caters for lunch and dinner, functions and private parties and corporate events.

Our culinary team of expert chefs will prepare your repast with care and on order for your enjoyment, from succulent seafood, through the highest quality meat and poultry dishes to a selection of special Portuguese dishes capped with original and tempting deserts.

Our service staff are highly trained, attentive and ready to meet your needs.

Please join us for a splendid repast, in a time honoured and graceful tradition. We look forward to welcoming you.
