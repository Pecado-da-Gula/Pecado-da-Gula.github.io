New Year’s Eve Dinner

On the evening of the 31st December we will be serving a 3 course spitbraai and buffet menu. Vegetarian options will be available. Booking is essential as we are limiting numbers and the price which includes a splendid repast, entertainment and dancing is R425 per head.

Live music will be provided by Vincent Du Chenne who many of you will know. Vincent is a well known vocalist, keyboardist, MC, Guest Speaker and recording artiste. He has performed, recorded, produced and accompanied most of South Africa’s top artists and is exceptional musician with an unbelievable ear for sound and the uncanny knack of being able to “read” the audience so you can be sure to be able to listen to and dance to your favourite numbers.

About Pecado da Gula

Pecado Da Gula is both a well known destination eatery and well kept secret amongst lovers of fine food and lovely venues. Set in rolling gardens under shady trees, the restaurant caters for lunch and dinner, functions and private parties and corporate events.

Our culinary team of expert chefs will prepare your repast with care and on order for your enjoyment, from succulent seafood, through the highest quality meat and poultry dishes to a selection of special Portuguese dishes capped with original and tempting deserts.

Our service staff are highly trained, attentive and ready to meet your needs.

Please join us for a splendid repast, in a time honoured and graceful tradition. We look forward to welcoming you.
